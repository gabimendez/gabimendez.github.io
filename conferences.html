Conference Tracker

The Conference Tracker is a resource designed to support faculty, administrators, and staff by providing a quick overview of selected conferences from around the world. It brings together key information such as conference modality (virtual, in-person, or hybrid), location, important dates, and potential fees, making it easier to explore opportunities at a glance.

The tracker focuses on conferences related to International Education, Global Learning, Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL), Virtual Exchange, Teaching and Learning, and other closely connected areas. These events play an important role in fostering collaboration, innovation, and the internationalization of education .

You are invited to explore the tracker, use it as a planning resource, and contribute by suggesting additional conferences or sharing feedback to help keep it relevant and useful for the community.

Note: Some dates currently listed are approximate, as official details for upcoming editions have not yet been announced—please double-check conference websites for confirmation. In some cases, submission deadlines may appear as closed based on the current timeline; these will be updated as new cycles are released.
