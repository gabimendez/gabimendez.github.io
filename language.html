Language Acknowledgement

About

A simple but meaningful way to recognize linguistic diversity in COIL and Virtual Exchange activities.

How to Use

  • Adapt to your context and audience
  • Use at the start of sessions or events
  • Include at least two languages when possible

Sample (English)

“Before we start our activities, we want to share that although the language used for this session will be English, we acknowledge and promote linguistic diversity. We recognize the effort involved in communicating in a foreign language and encourage all participants to speak from the heart and include expressions from their mother tongue.”

Ejemplo (Español)

“Antes de iniciar nuestras actividades, queremos compartir que aunque el idioma será el inglés, reconocemos la diversidad lingüística y animamos a participar desde el corazón.”
